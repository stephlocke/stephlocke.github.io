Biography

Steph Locke leads a life of data, coffee, books and board games. During the day, Steph’s a data scientist for CensorNet and works on implementing useful analytical solutions to keep organisations safe. In her spare time, Steph tries to help others work with data better by providing platforms for knowledge sharing and sharing her own knowledge.

Steph runs three user groups in Cardiff for SQL Server, R, and .Net, whilst supporting others to help her local area learn more. She used to help organise SQL Relay each year to help folks all around the country get access to free learning. She’s now working on a global series of conferences for R called satRdays, and a local conference Battle of the Beards. Steph blogs and presents, whenever she can, to share her own knowledge and experience. In recognition of her efforts, Microsoft recently made Steph a Microsoft Data Platform MVP for her contributions in the SQL and R worlds.
